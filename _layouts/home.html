Crisp Data

We provide high-quality data from many sources. No matter if you are looking for market data from Binance, Coinbase, Kraken: we got you covered. Enhance your predictions by incorporating on-chain data, like transaction costs and exchange in-/outflow, as well as social metrics derived from Twitter activity or Google search interest.

Visual Patterns

We believe that finding market patterns requires a strong visual approach and therefore focus on easy-to-compose graphs of selected metrics. A preview of the most recent data is presented for every selected timeseries and added to the graph of selected metrics. Find patterns, before you train your model.

Infinite Alpha

Combine data in multitudes of ways to guide our algorithms in search of patterns. Prepare our data in pre-specified ways or wire up your own data transformations according to your own needs. Choose from an extensive, ever expanding library of Machine- and Deep Learning algorithms. Use recommended parameters or tune your algorithm to your dataset.

Using AI has never been easier. Generate unique alpha and profit.

By training your customized model you choose which coins to trade and which risks you take. Publish predictions for cash and measure your performance with others. Become your own quant fund manager.
